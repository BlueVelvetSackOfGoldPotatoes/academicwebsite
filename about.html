About

I have a Bachelors and Masters in AI, having finished my bachelors with a thesis and an internship from the cardiology department of the UMCG, the Netherlands; and my Masters with a thesis carried out within the Pollice Research Group of the Stratingh Institute for Chemistry, University of Groningen, the Netherlands. I have developed expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) through various research and engineering roles across the Netherlands, Iceland, and Germany. My work spans academic research, industry-focused AI applications, and teaching in machine learning.
