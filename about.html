Current Roles

Co-Founder of Prismus.ai

  • What is Prismus: Prismus is a scientific intelligence system that converts fragmented technical sources—papers, books, datasets, codebases, instrument manuals/spec sheets, and live industry signals—into a single evidence-grounded, time-indexed knowledge graph: each document yields a verified “micro-graph” of methods/experiments/results/claims with precise provenance pointers, these are merged into a global “macro-graph” with typed citation roles, contradictions, and supersession over time, and the graph then powers graph- and temporal-reasoning tools (via API/MCP) that let users and agents navigate science by lenses, retrieve the exact supporting evidence for any statement, and generate grounded outputs plus forecasts (e.g., which labs will adopt which modalities/instruments next and why) that update as new evidence arrives. From this moat, we can spin off products and train models, that will hopefully help us solve many problems.
